## 🗓️ Sales Trends (Seasonality)
The business follows a **"Dual-Peak"** cycle:
1. **Summer Peak (July):** $1.04M (Driven by heat-related demand).
2. **Holiday Peak (December):** $1.04M (Driven by end-of-year retail surge).
3. **Q1 Trough:** Sales average below $500k/month, requiring new promotional strategies.

---

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations
* **Diversify Retail Partnerships:** Reduce the 50% dependency on *Sodapop* by expanding *BevCo* and *DreamCo* partnerships.
* **Inventory Optimization:** Scale stock in the West region starting in **May** to capture the July peak.
* **Q1 Refresh Campaign:** Launch targeted promotions in February to bridge the low-season revenue gap.
